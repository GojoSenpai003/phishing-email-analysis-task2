Phishing Email Analysis – Task 2

Sender: support@amaz0n-secure.com
Subject: URGENT: Your Amazon Account is Suspended

Findings:
- The sender's domain is fake and not the official Amazon domain.
- Uses urgency and fear tactics to manipulate the recipient.
- Hyperlink leads to a suspicious domain (amazon-secure-login.com) instead of the real amazon.com.
- Attachment appears to be a PDF but is actually an executable file (.exe).
- Greeting is generic (“Dear Customer”).
- There are spelling and grammar issues in the message.
- Return-Path and Reply-To headers mismatch during header analysis.

Conclusion:
This email is a phishing attempt that uses spoofing, misleading URLs, and malicious attachments.
